  • Our sofas and chairs are highly durable and built for longevity. So rest assured you can rely on the fact that our products will serve you well for years. If you want to make the most of your purchase, here are some tips to help you keep it as good as new for years.

    • Regularly dust your sofas and protect them from prolonged heat, moisture and direct sunlight exposure

    • Protect them from spillages of any kind and wipe off moisture promptly with a dry and soft cloth

    • Hardware, hinges etc., can be dusted with a soft brush

    • Metallic surfaces can be cleaned with a damp cloth

    • Avoid all kinds of acidic, abrasive and corrosive cleaning products

    • For retouching of any kind, do not use polish or paint products on your own. Only professional services are recommended

    • Keep sharp objects away that can cause potential scratches and other damage

    • Do not drag the product, always lift it from its base when you need to move it
